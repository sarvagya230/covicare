Vaccine Faq

+

You can log into the Co-WIN portal using the link www.cowin.gov.in and click on the “Register/Sign In yourself” tab to register for COVID-19 vaccination.

+

All beneficiaries aged 15 years and above (birth year 2007 or earlier) can register for vaccination

+

If you wish to check the list of nearby vaccination centres, you can do so using the CoWIN portal and the newly introduced feature in the WhatsApp chatbox. After visiting the homepage of the CoWIN website, you can access the find nearby vaccination centres feature and type your PIN code which will show a list of all the registered vaccination centres in the locality.

+

You can download vaccination certificate from the Co-WIN portal (www.cowin.gov.in) or the Aarogya Setu app or through Digi-Locker by following the simple steps. You may do so by using the mobile number used at the time of registration.
